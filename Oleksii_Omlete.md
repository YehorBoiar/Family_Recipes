# Oleksii's Omelet Recipe 

## Requirements

* Stainless steel pan
* Skill
* Spatula

## Ingredients

* High-heat oil (ideally Ghee)
* 2-3 Eggs
* 1 tsp Soy sauce
* Salt and pepper
* Butter
* Toppings (optional, e.g., cheese, dill)
* 1 tsp Water

## How to Make

1.  Combine the eggs, soy sauce, and 1 tsp of water in a bowl. 
2.  Whip the mixture using a fork **until consistent**.
3.  Pre-heat the pan to 160-240°C (test for the **Leidenfrost effect**).
4.  Add the ghee and spread it to cover the pan surface.
5.  Add the butter, wait until it browns slightly, and spread.
6.  Pour the egg mixture into the pan and let it set for a **little bit** (5-15 seconds).
7.  Reduce heat by a little bit.
8.  Use your spatula to gently push the cooked edges toward the center, "bunching up" the egg to create open space on the pan surface.
9.  Tilt the pan to let the remaining liquid egg flow into the empty areas formed in the previous step.
10. When there is no liquid egg left—stop!
